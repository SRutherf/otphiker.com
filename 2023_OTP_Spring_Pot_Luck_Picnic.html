2023 OTP SPRING POT LUCK PICNIC

In 2020, COVID forced cancelation of our annual party.
In 2021, despite our best efforts, resurging COVID concerns again killed our party plans.
In 2022, we had another excuse.

In 2023, we have an offer you cannot refuse.
A "FREE" pot-luck picnic lunch in Griffith Park.
Hikers, ex-hikers, spouses and partners are invited.

11:00 am - 3:00 pm
Wdnesday, April 19, 2023

Griffith Park, Crystal Springs Picnic Area
4730 Crystal Springs Drive, Los Angeles, CA 90027

Your West Valley party planners will host the event, reserve the venue,
and provide plates, utensils, napkins, cups, tableclothes and name tags.

Since there is no such thing as a free lunch,
all attendees are asked to bring food or drinks to share at the pot luck party,
so get out your favorite recipes and show off your talent.

Please send names of attendees and your pot luck dish to ...
Margie (mcarlin007@sbcglobal.net)
and Phil (email@philrutherford.com).
The table below will be updated daily based on your input.
Please check periodically to fill any gaps and ensure we do not have
a dozen bowls of potato salad.

Those bringing drinks or perishables please bring coolers with ice.
Additional chairs would also be welcome.

We are looking forward to seeing the whole OTP family again,
(hikers, inactive hikers, spouses and partners).

There will be no planned hike that day.
