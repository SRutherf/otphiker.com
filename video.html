OTP members have made great videos of OTP activities. Jim Carlin started making videos of hikes as early as 1996.  Dan Bergstrom has made a terrific set of videos of hikes that he has attended since April 2015. Dan also made videos for the 26th and 27th Anniversary Parties. Jim Hartung has made videos for the 25th and 28th Anniversary OTP Parties. 

All Dan's videos can be streamed from Vimeo. Videos of others can be streamed from YouTube.
