Directions to Trailhead:   The entrance to Malibu Creek State Park is accessed from Las Virgenes Road, 0.2 miles south of Mulholland Highway.  Those coming west on PCH should turn north on Malibu Canyon Road, which becomes Las Virgenes Road.  From Highway 101 going west, turn south on Las Virgenes Road.  There is an $11 parking fee for seniors and restrooms at the trailhead.

The Hike:  There are many trails in the park, and we have hiked over all of them.  Using GPS data in the table below and the linked Google Earth photo to indicate the leg locations/incremental distances, many loop hikes can be done from and back to the parking lot.
