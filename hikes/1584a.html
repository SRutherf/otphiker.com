Malaga Cove Traverse
August 21, 2024

Hike Leader:  Ron Obert

Meet at 8:00 am for start at 8:30 am
Rabbits will hike at an earlier time to be determined.

Basic Hike (magenta): RT = 6.8 miles; Cum Elevation = 810 ft.
Optional shorter or longer versions (green)

Directions to Trailhead:  The trailhead is the parking lot adjacent to the Peninsula Education Foundation office at 300 Paseo del Mar.  Reach the trailhead by exiting the 405 freeway and heading South on Hawthorne Blvd.  About half way up the Palos Verdes hill turn right on Palos Verdes Dr. North.  Continue West 2.4 miles to the triangular intersection and bear left as Palos Verdes Dr. transitions from North to West.  Continue a block through the Malaga Cove commercial district and turn right at Via Corta, which curves into Via Almar.  Turn right from Via Almar onto Via Arroyo and after a block turn right into the trailhead parking lot. Rest rooms are available to the South, across the expanse of grass.

The Hike: The Basic Hike makes a loop and returns to the trailhead.  The hike begins up the small road, transitions to a trail, and then onto city streets as it progresses in front of the Malaga Cove commercial district.  (From the open area look up the hill and you can see the cut for the fire road we will be on later.)  Continue on Via Tejon to a large open area, bounded by Palos Verdes Drive North and Palos Verdes Golf Course on the East.

There are a multitude of trails in this open area. Stay along the crest until you reach the golf course and their “Keep Out” signs.  Some may choose to return from here, but the main hike soon heads south up a heavy foliaged streambed, which is below power lines.  After crossing below the bridge for Via Campesina, turn right and continue on the Paseo del Sol fire road, (it’s the cut seen earlier from below).

Continue as Paseo del Sol turns from a fire road to a real road and crosses Via del Monte.  The Basic Hike continues up Paseo del Sol and turns downward on the trail after 1821 Paseo del Sol.  The trail progresses to Palos Verdes Dr. West.  Turn right, as the hike follows the median until it ends.  Cross Palos Verdes Dr. West to the small parking lot and “Viewpoint”.  Continue North East on the trail as it parallels Palos Verdes Drive West.  After about half a mile its  probably best to cross a brushy canyon on a walkway next to the Drive.  Very soon after the walkway, drop down to  Paseo Del Mar.  Another quarter of a mile will expose a wide open area of the bluff, which is the start of the Bluff Cove Trail which leads down to the sea.  After returning from the Cove, Paseo Del Mar will be mainly residential street but partially bluff trail, as it returns us to our trailhead.

Shorter Hikes: The first of the two shorter hike possibilities saves distance as we  approach the Palos Verdes Golf Club.  The second and major hike shortner continues down Via Del Monte, as the basic hike transitions from Del Sol Fire Road to Paseo Del Sol.  It soon passes La Venta Inn, a well known wedding and party venue. Continue through the La Venta Inn parking lot and merge with Via Somonte until, across from 820 Via Somonte take the downward path and stairs. Walk up Via del Monte and turn right and follow Via Arriba, and then the path and downward stairs across from 1704 Via Arriba.  At the bottom of the stairs turn hard right and follow the path back to the Malaga Cove commercial district, and then back to the trailhead. 

Longer Hikes: For a longer hike, from the viewpoint on Palos Verdes Drive West turn left onto the Blufftop Trail, for an additional three quarters of mile and then return.  For another option for additional distance descend on the small paved road just past the trailhead parking lot.  This leads past the Palos Verdes Beach and Athletic Club to the Malaga Cove Trail and then to RAT Beach. 

Party on the nice grass under large shade trees adjacent to the parking lot.  There are no tables or benches, so bringing folding chairs would be a good idea.
