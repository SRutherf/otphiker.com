Orange County Hike
Peters Canyon Regional Park
December 23, 2020

Meet at 9:00 am

GPS Data:
6 miles RT. 1,000 feet elevation gain.

Directions to Trailhead:  Exit east from the 55 freeway  at Chapman in Orange.  Turn right onto Canyon View after 2.25 miles.  Continue 2 miles on Canyon View to the park on the right.  Address is 8548 E. Canyon View Ave., Orange, CA 92869. You will need to park in the parking lot where there is a $3 parking fee.

The Hike: Our hike will be a loop starting East from the parking lot and continuing South on a hilly ridge to the south end of the park.  Then we return to the North on the trail on the West side of the park.
