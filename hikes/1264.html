Hike #1264
Chantry Flat Winter Creek Loop
March 9, 2016

Wendell Suzuki, Hike Leader

GPS Data: (See Below)

There are many hike choices from this trailhead: It could be an out and back to one of the camps along Gabrielino Trail and return. Example: Return from Spruce Grove or turn around sooner.
It could be a longer loop trip up to Sturdevant Campground then onto Mount Zion and return via Upper or Lower Winter Creek trails.
It could be a shorter loop from Roberts Camp along Lower Winter Creek to Hoegee Campground and return via Upper Winter Creek.
It could be a long out and back on the Santa Anita Ridge Trail, or even a longer trek extending from the ridge along the Olene Ridge and back via Upper Winter Creek.
Or it could be a shorter descent to Hermit Falls starting from the "First Water" trail turnoff.

Directions to Trailhead: Take the 210 FREEWAY east to SANTA ANITA AVENUE exit (turns into Santa Anita Canyon road) and drive north to Chantry Flats, about 5-6 miles. Park in the upper large lot. Signed trailhead is across the road. Restroom facility is available. Don't forget Forestry parking pass.

Weather and altitude thoughts: Chantry = 2,220 feet, Mt. Zion = 3,575 feet, Spruce Grove = 3,100 feet, Hoegees = 2,500 feet, Sturtevant = 3,240 feet. Hopefully all below snow level, but wouldn’t it be nice to see some?
