Septeember 4, 2024
Will Rogers to Temescal via Rivas Canyon

Hike Leader: Dorothy King

Meet at 8:00 am for start at 8:30 am

Please print your own hike descriptions and maps..

Distance: 5.8 miles RT
Elevation Gain: 954 ft.

Directions to Trailhead: Valley folks can take Topanga Canyon Road to PCH, and then south to Sunset Blvd. About 3 miles up Sunset turn left on Will Rogers State Park Road. Alternately, take the 405 freeway to Sunset Blvd, go west about 6 miles, and then right on Will Rogers State Park Road.

Coming from the south, take PCH to Sunset Blvd. and follow instructions above. Note that there is a parking fee or you can use a pass from the California State Park Foundation.

The Hike: To get to the trailhead, follow the road down past the Parking Kiosk. Go right on the 1st road; go past 2 houses and cross the field diagonally to the entrance to the trail. Alternatively, take the Rivas Canyon Trailhead at the east end of the Kiosk parking lot and bear left. There are very few trail markers along the way. At the bottom of the canyon, cross the road and continue the hike on the dirt trail. Later, turn left at the sign that says "SMMNRA Hiking Trail - Unsafe for Horses". It’s quite safe for humans.  After the open area at the top of your climb, you will reach a fork.  Stay to the right and continue on down to Temescal Canyon Gateway Park.

Much of the trail is shaded. Warning: watch out for poison oak. There are bathrooms at both parks. The bathroom at Temescal is to the left once you reach the road.  It is downhill a little ways near the parking lot.

For those wanting additional hiking distance, you can choose to hike the Temescal Canyon trail to the waterfall, or hike on the Temescal Ridge Trail. Alternately, you can go back to Will Rogers & hike around the grounds or do the Inspiration Point Trail. The Temescal waterfall is listed as 2.6 miles round trip from Temescal Gateway Park. The Will Rogers Inspiration Point Loop Trail is an additional 1.7 miles round trip.

There is a very interesting video about Will Rogers in a building behind the Ranch House from 10 AM to 4 PM, and tours of the Ranch are at 11:00 AM, 1:00 & 2:00 PM. .
