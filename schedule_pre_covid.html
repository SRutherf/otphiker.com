HIKE LEADERS:  To get your hike description posted on this page send it by e-mail to the Hike Coordinator at least two weeks prior to the hike date.  If the Hike Coordinator link is not working, copy and paste the following into the To: box of your email app ... hikecoordinator@otphiker.com
