RABBITS' HIKE SCHEDULE
Operated During the COVID-19 Pandemic

During the COVID-19 pandemic, regular OTP hikes were cancelled. However local hikes were conducted with smaller regional groups.

The Rabbits conducted hikes in the San Gabriel Mountains, the Verdugo Mountains and occasionally in the Santa Monica Mountains. Hikes generally started at 7:00 am every Wednesday.

Active members participating in these hikes included Steve Babcock, Dan Bergstrom, Mike Diilenback, Rich Frederick, Sean Frederick, Tom Kaczmarek, Rick Martin, Ralph Shoukry and Wendell Suzuki, Steve Babcock coordinated the hikes..

Links below are generally to the previous regular OTP hike descriptions done before the COVID-19 shutdown.  These regional hikes were not assigned a new OTP hike #.  Several new hikes link to AllTrails descriptions.
