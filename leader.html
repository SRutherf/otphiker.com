UPCOMING HIKE LEADERS

The leader list gives your progress towards leading a hike.  Every time you attend a hike you receive one point and progress up the list.  When you lead a hike, you loose points based on the 6-month rolling average of the hike attendance (currently 17 hikers).  In this way, everyone rotates hike leader responsibilities based on the frequency of their hike attendance. See Leader List Procedure for more detail.

If you're in the top five, it's time to plan your hike.  Please send your chosen hike and date via e-mail to the Webmaster.
