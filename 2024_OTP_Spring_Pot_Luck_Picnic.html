2024 OTP SPRING POTLUCK PICNIC


Last Year's Picnic - April 19, 2023

The South Bay will host the 2nd annual potluck picnic lunch in Griffith Park.
Hikers, ex-hikers, spouses and partners are invited.

11:00 am - 3:00 pm
Wednesday, April 17, 2024

Griffith Park, Crystal Springs Picnic Area
4730 Crystal Springs Drive, Los Angeles, CA 90027

Your South Bay party planners will host the event, reserve the venue,
and provide plates, utensils, napkins, cups, tableclothes and name tags.

All attendees are asked to bring food or drinks to share at the potluck party,
so get out your favorite recipes and show off your talent.

Please use the SignUpGenius online sign-up website below
to RSVP and state your potluck dish
and the number of attendees in your party.
SignUpGenius

Those bringing drinks or perishables please bring coolers with ice.
Please note that no alcohol is allowed in city parks.
Additional chairs would also be welcome.

We are looking forward to seeing the whole OTP family again,

There will be no official hike that day.
