WET WEATHER HIKE OPTIONS

Occaisionally, following days of heavy rain, it may become necessary to make a last minute change to a scheduled hike to avoid potential or unknown muddy trail conditions. The following are potential options that the hike leader could choose, based on either known trails of decomposed granite or street hikes.  The hike leader will need to notify the group via OTPHiker@groups.io of the change no later than noon on Tuesday, plus notify the webmaster so that changes to the website schedule can be made.

Acceptable Trail Conditions

  • Verdugo Mountains - Beaudry Loop
  • Griffith Park - Fern Dell Trailhead
  • Bonelli Park, Puddingstone Reservoir
  • Crystal Cove Beach
  • Black Star Canyon, Silverado

Street Walks

  • LA River Greenway
  • Mount Washington
  • Echo Park / Silverlake Stairs
  • Venice Canals
  • Naples, Long Beach
