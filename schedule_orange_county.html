ORANGE COUNTY HIKE SCHEDULE
Operated During the COVID-19 Pandemic

During the COVID-19 pandemic, regular OTP hikes were cancelled. However local hikes were conducted with smaller regional groups.

Orange County conducted hikes in Orange County, Rancho Palos Verdes and the San Gabriel Mountains.

Active members participating in these hikes included Mickey Costello, Bob De Wolf, Dennis Fassnacht, Jan Gardner, Jim Gardner, Tom Kaczmarek, Dorothy King, Randy King, John Morton,Veronica Noguer, Sam Prum, Larry Savell and Bob Singer.  Bob De Wolf coordinated the hilkes.

Links below are generally to the previous regular OTP hike descriptions done before the COVID-19 shutdown.  These local hikes were not assigned a new OTP hike #.
